A paragraph consists of one or more sentences that form a self-contained unit of discourse. The start of a paragraph is indicated by a new line.

Text is easier to understand when it is split up into units of text. For example, a book may have chapters. Chapters can have subheadings. Under each heading there will be one or more paragraphs.

Lunar New Year usually falls between late January and early February; It is one of the most important holidays in Vietnam. Officially, the festival includes the 1st, 2nd, 3rd lunar days; however, Vietnamese people usually spend nearly a month to celebrate this special event. New Year's Day begins with the first day of the Lunar Year; however, its preparation began long before that. The 23rd day of the first lunar month is the Eastern day - the worshiping ceremony of Tao Quan (Tao Cong). It is said that every year on this day, these gods ascend to heaven to inform the Jade Emperor of all the activities of earthly households. On New Year's Eve, they return home to continue their family duty. On New Year's Day, the first people to visit households - known as the forerunners - are very important and therefore should be chosen carefully, as they believe that they will hold the whole of their luck. family in the new year (Tan Nien). Then, on the 3rd and even 4th of the Lunar New Year, people meet with relatives, friends, and colleagues, wishing for good things such as happiness, health and success.
